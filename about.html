Features
  • The first plot depicts the publications of an individual. The publications are represented in a 2D diagram (number of citations vs. year of publication) with the h-index line. The journals are represented as disks with area analogous to the impact factor the journal. The patents are represented by cross and the books/conferences by triangles. By pointing at a symbol you can obtain the publication’s title, the number of citations, the journal/conference name and its impact factor (if it is a journal). The journals, patents and conferences/books can be selectively viewed by clicking on the legend. The plot in Log10 scale is shown below.



    • Same plot in Decimal scale is shown below.




  • The second plot depicts the NSF and NIH funding of an individual as a multiline. Each breakpoint in the multiline corresponds to the individual’s total amount in new NSF/NIH awards for the specific year. By pointing at a breakpoint you can obtain the NSF/NIH award(s) ID(s), award amount(s), and investigator’s role(s). Total funding information per year is also available by clicking the legend.
